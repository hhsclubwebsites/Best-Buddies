What is Best Buddies?

Best Buddies fosters connections with those with intellectual and developmental disabilties. Our goal is to create a welcoming and inclusive environment with individuals with IDD by creating meaningful friendships and hosting different events.

How do I join?

Joining Best Buddies is simple! You just need to attend at least 3 meetings a trimester to be considered a member. There are usually 2 meetings a month - in school meetings are 45 minutes while out of school meetings are usually an hour and a half.
